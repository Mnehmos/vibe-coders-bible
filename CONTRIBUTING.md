# Contributing

This project is a field manual and book manuscript. Contributions are strongest when they are specific, reproducible, and tied to a real workflow.

## Good contributions

- A case study of an AI-assisted change that failed review, tests, or production.
- A control that moves risk higher in the hierarchy of controls.
- A template that teams can copy into a repo and use immediately.
- A correction that makes an existing recommendation more precise.
- A chapter expansion under `manuscript/chapters/` that preserves the book thesis.

## Contribution workflow

1. Open or claim an issue when the change is more than a typo.
2. Keep PRs narrow enough to review in one sitting.
3. Prefer concrete examples over abstract advice.
4. Link related chapters, templates, or case studies.
5. Use the PR checklist before requesting review.

## Manuscript workflow

- Keep chapter titles aligned with `manuscript/MANIFEST.md`.
- Preserve the spine: AI makes detail affordable, not unnecessary.
- Put reusable checklists and prompts in `templates/`.
- Keep release-process notes in `publishing/`.
- Avoid self-referential revision language such as "this version" or "we added."

## Writing style

- Be direct.
- Avoid tool worship. Tools change; controls survive.
- Name failure modes clearly.
- Separate evidence from intuition.
- Prefer "do this in the repo" over "remember to be careful."
