# Chapter 4 - Trust AI To Propose

Part: II - Trust, But Verify

## Thesis

The correct relationship to AI is not distrust, worship, or micromanagement. It is controlled delegation.

## Key line

The model is allowed to be creative before the gate. It is not allowed to be authoritative after the gate.

## Chapter job

Define AI as proposer, human as intent owner, and tests, schemas, and tools as the verification substrate.

## Main beats

- AI as proposer.
- Human as intent owner.
- The repo as the shared operating context.
- Tests, schemas, tools, and review as verification substrate.
- Why "never trust AI" is too crude.
- Why "just ship it" is reckless.
