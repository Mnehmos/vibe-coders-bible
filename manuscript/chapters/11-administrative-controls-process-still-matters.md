# Chapter 11 - Administrative Controls: Process Still Matters

Part: III - The Hierarchy Of AI Controls

## Thesis

Checklists and workflows cannot replace engineering controls, but they still reduce chaos.

## Key line

Process is not the safety system. Process is how humans remember to use the safety system.

## Chapter job

Place process correctly. Administrative controls help coordinate humans and agents, but they should point toward concrete evidence.

## Main beats

- Issue-first development.
- Branch naming.
- PR templates.
- Definition of done.
- Changelogs.
- Release notes.
- Architecture decision records.
- Agent handoff files.
- Review lanes.
