# Chapter 37 - The Safety Checklist

Part: VIII - The Field Manual

## Thesis

A safety checklist is most useful when organized by the hierarchy of controls.

## Key line

Start with hazards removed, then tools narrowed, then checks automated, then process, then attention.

## Chapter job

Turn the hierarchy into a reusable checklist.

## Checklist structure

- Eliminated hazards.
- Substituted safe tools.
- Engineering controls.
- Administrative controls.
- PPE and prompting.
