# Chapter 36 - The Agent Handoff Protocol

Part: VIII - The Field Manual

## Thesis

Agent work fails when the next human or agent cannot tell what changed, why it changed, what passed, and what remains risky.

## Key line

A handoff is a trace written for the next mind.

## Chapter job

Define the minimum useful handoff for AI-assisted work.

## Required handoff fields

- What changed.
- Why it changed.
- What files matter.
- What tests pass or fail.
- What remains risky.
- What the next agent should do.
- What not to touch.
