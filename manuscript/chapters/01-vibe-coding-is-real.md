# Chapter 1 - Vibe Coding Is Real

Part: I - The New Discipline

## Thesis

Vibe coding is not fake. It is a real change in the labor economics of software.

## Key line

Vibe coding lowers the cost of generation. It does not lower the cost of responsibility.

## Chapter job

Name the cultural shift without mocking it or romanticizing it. Natural language has become a software interface, and nontraditional builders can now ship. The chapter should respect that new power while drawing a hard line between generation and ownership.

## Main beats

- Natural language as a practical software interface.
- AI as a labor multiplier, not a replacement for accountability.
- Why nontraditional builders can now ship meaningful systems.
- Why experienced developers underestimate the cultural shift.
- Why beginners overestimate what the machine understands.
- The difference between making software appear and making software reliable.
