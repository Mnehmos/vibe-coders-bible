# Chapter 12 - PPE: Prompting, Style, And Human Attention

Part: III - The Hierarchy Of AI Controls

## Thesis

Prompting is useful, but it is the last layer.

## Key line

Prompt discipline improves behavior. It does not enforce behavior.

## Chapter job

Give useful prompting patterns while explaining their limits. The reader should use prompts as part of a system, not as a substitute for one.

## Main beats

- Good prompts.
- Role prompts.
- Constraints.
- Examples.
- "Ask before destructive actions."
- "Show plan before edit."
- "Do not invent APIs."
- "Use tests."
- Why these are insufficient alone.

## Practical artifact

Add a prompt checklist tied to controls:

- Does this prompt define intent?
- Does it name scope?
- Does it name validation?
- Does it require a handoff?
- Does a system control enforce the critical parts?
