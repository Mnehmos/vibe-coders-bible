# Chapter 3 - The New Bottleneck Is Judgment

Part: I - The New Discipline

## Thesis

Once generation becomes cheap, judgment becomes the scarce resource.

## Key line

AI gives you more attempts. It does not tell you which attempt deserves to become real.

## Chapter job

Explain why more output does not automatically mean more progress. The new bottleneck is deciding what to accept, what to reject, what to test, and what to preserve.

## Main beats

- Why more code can become more review debt.
- The review bottleneck.
- The context bottleneck.
- The "looks right" trap.
- Plausible wrongness as the central AI failure mode.
- Taste, product sense, architecture, and operational judgment.

## Practical artifact

Create a commit judgment checklist:

- What changed?
- Why should this change exist?
- What evidence supports it?
- What would make it wrong while still looking right?
- What changed outside the expected scope?
- What must future maintainers understand?

## Draft notes

This chapter sets up Part II. The reader should leave convinced that judgment must be routed through artifacts, not just intuition.
